2 Short Story

Jeff was a 15-year-old who moved to a new neighborhood with his brother, Liu, and their parents. Jeff never had seen the neighborhood before
Jeff and his parents not had a good relationship. They were always in their worlds.
On their first day of school, they were accosted by 3 bullies named Randy, Keith, & Troy, whom Jeff brutally beat up when they threatened them with knives.

Liu took the blame for it and was arrested, sending Jeff into a deep depression. Jeff loved his brother very much.
The bullies days later. attacked Jeff again at a party, and Jeff killed one of them, but not before getting covered in bleach and lit on fire by Keith. During this fight, Jeff's mind permanently snapped.
